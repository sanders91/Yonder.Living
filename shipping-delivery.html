Here at YONDER.living we aim to dispatch all our orders within 3 working days, although this may be longer during particularly busy periods. However, our team will be sure to keep you updated on the progress of your order. You will also receive an email of confirmation as soon as your order has been placed. Information on delivery methods, charges and times can be found further on this page.


DELIVERY METHODS.

All orders are sent using Royal Mail or by courier service Hermes. Deliveries will require a signature upon delivery, so if you are not able to be at home for a delivery, then we may suggest sending your order somewhere in which you or someone you trust will be able to sign for your package. For international orders Royal Mail work in conjunction with the local postal service within each country.
